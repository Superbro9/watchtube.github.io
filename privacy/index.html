Privacy

We do not collect any data.

Scroll down for more





Really?

Yep! Our app doesn't collect any user data at all!

We do not gather data because it is not needed. We rely on users to tell us everything we need and bug reports must be manually sent to us, either through our community Discord server, or our Twitter account.

Usage data may be collected by third parties such as those who host Invidious instances which WatchTube utilises.

We are not responsible for anything that goes wrong if you use a non-recommended instance.

We recommend only using the instances listed in WatchTube as they're trusted by Invidious, but you can press and hold the instance picker to choose a custom instance to use.
