Privacy

Really?

Yep! Our app doesn't collect any user data at all!

We do not gather data because it's not needed. We rely on users to tell us anything and bug reports must be manually sent to us.

Usage may be collected by third parties such as those who host Invidious instances which WatchTube utilises.

We are not responsible for anything that goes wrong if you use an instance.

We recommend only using the instances listed in WatchTube as they're trusted by Invidious, but you can hold the instance picker to choose a custom instance to use.
