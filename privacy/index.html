Privacy
WatchTube is a privacy-respecting application.
No data is sent off to external servers of any kind. Any user data and gathered user activities are stored on-device and are processed on-device to provide a good experience whilst keeping your data private and safe.

Our servers are used to answer your queries, such as searching and getting metadata. None of this traffic is identifiable to you specifically and our servers do not log any data linked to you. Servers will generate logs containing debugging information about traffic but won't contain personally-identifying information or similar.
